# Regulatory and Compliance Strategy

## Intended Use

The system provides triage risk support to licensed clinicians. It does not autonomously assign triage priority and does not replace clinical judgment.

## CDS and SaMD Boundary

Regulatory review must determine whether the tool qualifies as non-device CDS or Software as a Medical Device. The risk is higher because triage recommendations can influence time-sensitive care.

## Required Controls

- HIPAA-compliant hosting
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Role-based access control
- Audit logging
- Model versioning
- Human override workflow
- Model card
- Data sheet
- Change-control review

## Safety Governance

A clinical AI governance committee should review performance, safety events, fairness reports, and proposed model updates quarterly.
